About Robert Downey Jr.

Robert Downey Jr. is an American actor and producer. He is best known for his roles as Iron Man/Tony Stark in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, as well as for his performances in films such as Chaplin, Tropic Thunder, and Sherlock Holmes.

Awards and Nominations

  • 2 Academy Award nominations
  • 2 Golden Globe wins and 4 nominations
  • 1 BAFTA win and 2 nominations
  • 4 Screen Actors Guild Awards
  • 1 Emmy Award nomination
